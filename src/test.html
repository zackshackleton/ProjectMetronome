If the conductor and metronome/battery (yellow dot) are visually together, anyone within the same ring as the metronome/battery may play with the conductor. For each ring outwards from the one which contains the battery/metronome, a player must anticipate the conductor by one note of the value selected for acceptable error. For each ring inwards from the battery/metronome, a player must play one note of the value selected for acceptable error behind the conductor. Anyone within the "green zone" may play with what they hear from the battery/metronome with negligible effect on time.
